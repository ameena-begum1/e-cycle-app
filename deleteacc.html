Request Account and Data Deletion

If you would like to delete your Ecycle account and associated data, please send an email to:

ecycle.app@gmail.com

Make sure to email us using the same address associated with your Ecycle account. Include the subject line: Account Deletion Request.

What will be deleted?

  • Your account and login credentials
  • Personal information linked to your account
  • All associated usage data

Data may be retained for up to 30 days only if required by law or for security reasons.
